World Shipping Port Analysis

I developed a World Shipping Port Analysis dashboard, show that there was 73,590 total vessels, the country with the most vessels in ports is China with 34,418 and second is the USA with 5,853. The most common vessels by port type were Port with 61,383, the Anchorage with 10,199, and then Mariana, and Canal with 1,241 and 767 respectively. The 3 Countries with the most ports are China, the USA, and the Netherlands. China was the country with the most port arrivals compared to any country at 26,714 arrivals, almost 10 times higher than the country with the second highest arrivals by country.

Health Insurance Coverage Analysis Dashboard

Using Tableau I developed a Health Insurance Coverage Analysis dashboard, I completed an exploratory data analysis project showing that California, Texas and New York were the states with the highest employer health insurance by state with 19.5 million dollars, 13.7 million dollars, and 10.8 million dollars respectively. California had the highest health insurance change coverage by state with 3.8 million dollars. California also was the state with the highest Medicaid enrolment by state at 11.8 million almost doubling the next state with the highest Medicaid enrolment which was New York. Alaska had the highest monthly tax credit at $750. Texas has the highest uninsured rate going from 0.237 to 0.171, and Nevada has the biggest change going from 0.226 to 0.123.

Shark Attacks Analysis Dashboard

This is a detailed dashboard displaying the Shark Attacks across the world. There were 6,096 total shark attacks across the globe from 2005 to 2016, with the 81% of shark attacks being male and 19% being female. There were 4,393 non-fatal shark attacks, and 1,566 fatal shark attacks over the years. 2015 had the highest shark attacks with 141. The majority of shark attacks that occurred between 2005 and 2015 were unprovoked. The activities that resulted in the most shark attacks were Swimming with 879, Surfing with 935, and Fishing with 428. The countries that had the most attacks was USA, Australia, and South Africa, with 2,160, 1,303, and 571 respectively, this is a result of these countries being surrounded by large bodies of water.
